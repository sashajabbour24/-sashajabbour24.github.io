1.1.9 project: Who Wants to be a Millionaire?

My partner and I made a replication of the game show, "Who Wants to be a Millionaire?" The player is asked a series of questions and gets a certain amount of money if they get the question correct. If they answer incorrectly they lose all their money.


1.2.5 project: Archery Range

My partner and I made an archery range game. The player chooses a difficulty and they try to hit the moving targets. Each target you hit gives you a point and you have a timer running. After the timer runs up, the scoreboard will appear.


Scratch Project: Pizzaria Eatery

My partner and I made a recreation of a Pizzeria game on Scratch. A costumer will come to the table and ask for a certain pizza. You will act as a pizza chef and make the pizza for the costumer. You can collect money from the costumers.
